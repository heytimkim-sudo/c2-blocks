C2
Musical Play Instrument · Prototype

C2 is a simulation of a physical modular music toy. Wooden blocks connect magnetically — their arrangement makes music. This is the digital prototype. Pick up a block and start.

THE BLOCKS
🥁 K · S · H · B
The four sound blocks — Kick, Snare, Hi-Hat, Bass. Tap to play. Connect two or more and they loop in sequence.
✛ D-PAD
Snap to a chain to switch instruments. ↑ / ↓ cycles through Drums · Piano · Strings · Horns · Guitar. Works live mid-loop.
🕹 JOYSTICK
Snap to a chain to activate a live filter. Left/Right = cutoff frequency. Up/Down = resonance. Stick springs back to center.
🟫 MAT
Drop blocks onto the mat. The distance between them controls pitch — closer = higher, further = lower. Drag blocks while playing to sweep pitch live.
CONNECTING
Snap Drag a block near any other block — it glows gold and clicks into place.
Insert Drag toward the connector between two joined blocks — it glows white. Drop to slot in mid-chain.
Group Grab any block in a chain — the whole chain moves together.
Detach Drag a block away from its chain — it separates on first movement.
Delete Hover a block → red ✕ appears → click. Or right-click.
PLAYING
Loop Hit ▶ PLAY. Only chains of 2+ blocks loop — solo blocks stay silent. Mat blocks always play.
Tap Click any block at any time to trigger it, even during a loop.
Strum In Guitar mode — grab a block and shake it side to side. Each pass strums one string.
BPM Drag the slider in the top bar to change tempo (60–200 BPM).
CANVAS
Zoom Scroll wheel · or + / − buttons (bottom-right)
Pan Middle mouse drag · or hold Space + drag
Reset ⌖ button to return to default view
TRY THIS
1 Snap K → S → H → B in a row and hit Play for an instant beat.
2 Attach the D-Pad and press ↑ while playing to switch to Piano.
3 Add the Joystick. Sweep it left and right while the loop runs.
4 Drop a Mat. Put all four blocks on it. Drag them apart while playing.
5 Switch to Guitar. Shake a block side to side to strum a chord.
